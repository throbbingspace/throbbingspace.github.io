FinnCycling-Soumi-Perkele! (2017)

Description

FinnCycling-Soumi-Perkele! is a "spinning"-type exercise bike class that explores topics such as work and Finnish austerity politics. The viewer can take part in the exercise programme led by the artist Martta Tuomaala, by cycling on one of the exercise bikes available. The participatory artwork is part of Martta Tuomaala’s broader multidisciplinary project that explores work-related topics and the role and position of women in Finnish society.

Details

  • Video installation
  • 60 min.
  • HD 1920x1080p
  • Stereo 2.0

Terttu Järvelä: Sotkamon Saatana (2017)

Description

During August 2017 we filmed a music video for the song "Sotkamon Saatana" by Finnish rap artist Terttu Järvelä. The video, as well as the song itself, critizes the disasters the Finnish Talvivaara mining company has done to our nature with its neglecting of proper safety measures.

Most of the documentary footage was received from an activist group Stop Talvivaara!, and some was shot by myself during a filming trip to the mining area. Additional fictional footage was shot during midnight in a completely dark forest, with a crew consisting only me (camera, lights) and the director Martta Tuomaala.

My roles

  • Assistant producer
  • Cinematographer
  • Editor
  • Motion graphics designer
